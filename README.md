# WallBuilder

**WallBuilder** is a simple Quality of Life [tModLoader](https://github.com/tModLoader/tModLoader) mod for Terraria, designed to take the tedium out of construction.

## Features

- **3 Tiers of Wall Wands** — Basic, Advanced, and Master, each offering increased range and capability.
- **Instant Coverage** — Fills background walls in a large area with a single click.
- **Smart Enclosure Detection** — Automatically fills enclosed spaces like houses or rooms, stopping perfectly at solid blocks, platforms, and doors without spilling outside.
- **Smart Biome Adaptation** — Automatically detects your current biome and chooses the matching wood/wall type.
- **Builder Focused** — Optimized for fast arena setups and large house building.

## Installation

### Steam Workshop
Subscribe to the mod on the Steam Workshop, then enable it from the tModLoader Mods menu in-game.

## Known Issues

- Wall placement underground in specific biomes may place an unsafe wall type, which can increase monster spawn rates on farms built there. A fix for biome-aware underground wall selection is planned.
